Our Features

Outstanding Community to Learn and Explore things with Ultimate Features.!!

Youngovators is built for Young Kids like you.
With useful features, an intuitive interface.

Peer to Peer Learning

Peer to peer learning allows students who may be located very far from one another to build a global network.

Doubt Addressing System

By cultivating the habit of asking doubts, and resolving them, students develop critical thinking.

National Level Competitions

It helps students to develop their creativity and their skill sets.

Regular Quiz

The daily quiz provides a more-focused review of class topics; it provides a sharper focus in terms of assessing cognitive learning.

Project/Content Repository

Content Repository or content store is a database of digital content with an associated set of data management

Expert Sessions

Expert sessions are an opportunity for delegates to sit down and converse on a more one-to-one level with the experts in their field.

Social Proofing

Social proof works by tapping into the basic human instinct to follow the actions of others, building trust and credibility.

Project Showcase

It provides an opportunity to learn from each other and to appreciate the work of others and a strong sense of classroom community.

Code Editor

The code editor is a text edit area that allows developers to write, edit and save a document of code.
